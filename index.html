Bass Guitar

Bass Guitar, electric bass guitar or what some people would like to call it simply bass is a low-pitch instrument. it is very similar to an electric guitar except the fact that the bass guitar has only the top 4 strings. It is advised that if you know how to play an electric guitar, you will know bass guitar straight away.

Electric Guitar

Guitar is more of the lead of the instrument as it has more notes than what the bass guitar has and it is basically also similar to a piano the guitar is also used to be playing solos and gives much of a feeling when playing and also carries the song from the beginning till the end.

Piano

Piano, almost like a guitar, but except that you are sitting down. It also carries the songs as well, but it's more of like a semi leader if the electric guitar player is not there to lead the melody, then the piano player can lead. It is also fun when trying to change up the song of the piano allowing the muscician to play any other instrument.

Drum Kit

The drums is one of the main instruments, more time what people would say the easiest. The drum kit gives a beat and keep everyone else on time. In addition, the drum kit even helps out to create more of a mood to the song as hitting the other parts of the drums creates a nice sound. Furthermore, most players that lay drums are mostly people that can multi-task as it requires you to move your hands and legs at the same time, whilst staying on time with the beat.
